
Case Study: Bariatric Clinic Activity Trends

 

Situation: The Chief Financial Officer (CFO) has noticed reports of decreased bariatric surgery volume nationwide and has asked the Program Director to provide feedback on trends within the bariatric clinic at Cedars-Sinai Marina Hospital.

 

The Office Manager has provided data from our Enterprise Information Services (EIS) regarding all clinic activity between 2021 - 2025 (Excel spreadsheet attached.)
 

As the Operations Analyst for the Bariatric Clinic, you are tasked with leading the kickoff meeting. Those in attendance include:
 

Medical Director
Program Director
Office Manager
 

The ask of you, as the potential new Operations Analyst, is to prepare and anticipate presenting the following for your upcoming interview:

 

Synthesizing the Clinic data and identifying any trends.
 

Delivering a 5–10-minute slide deck presentation sharing your findings.
 

Outlining the next steps for the project.
